THE HOUSE

581 posts from #walterwhitehouse
3828 Piermont Dr, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87112 USA

Wanna rage like Walter White? Then this is the activity for you. At the iconic White House you’ll be able to break bad and throw the authentic unsliced pizza on the house roof. The thrill doesn’t stop here: you may encounter “The Lady” the super nice cursing owner (in that case be ready to run). For the best experience we recommend buying a party pizza at Gino’s.

WHAT TO BRING:

- Gino’s party pizza one or more, in case you miss
- running shoes
- extra cash for some blue sky

PRICE:

Gino's NY Style Party Pizza: 25€
